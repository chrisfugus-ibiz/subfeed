SubFeed

Your YouTube subscriptions, newest first.
No algorithm. No recommendations. Just your channels.

Get started in 3 steps
1
Go to your YouTube subscriptions feed
SubFeed activates automatically on youtube.com/feed/subscriptions. No setup needed — just navigate there and SubFeed takes over.
Open subscriptions feed ↗
2
Your feed is now chronological
Videos from every channel you follow are shown newest-first, with zero recommended content injected between them. A green control bar appears at the top of your feed.
3
Customise with the toolbar icon
Click the SubFeed icon in your Chrome toolbar to change the time window (last 24h, 3 days, 7 days), toggle Calm mode to remove thumbnails, or hide YouTube Shorts entirely.
What SubFeed does
Chronological order
Newest uploads always appear first. No engagement-based reordering.
Subscriptions only
Zero recommended videos, suggested channels, or sponsored rows injected into your feed.
Time window filter
See only what's new in the last 24h, 3 days, or 7 days. Catch up fast.
Calm mode
Strip thumbnails for a distraction-free, text-only feed. No visual clickbait.
Hide Shorts
Remove all YouTube Shorts from your subscription feed for a cleaner experience.
Live stats
See how many subscriptions you have, how many videos are new today, and ads blocked.
Your privacy is the product — not you
  • No account required — SubFeed never asks for a login or email
  • Zero data collection — all settings stored locally in your browser only
  • No server — SubFeed has no backend; nothing leaves your device
  • No tracking — we don't know who you are or what you watch
  • Open source — audit every line of code on GitHub
